Albert Einstein

A tribute to the greatest scientist of all Time.

Albert Einstein (Ulma 1879 – Princeton 1955) was a theoric physicist. He he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century and one of the greatest innovator of the past century. His work were seminal for a great part of the physics study in the XX century as his study on photoelectric effect originating the quantistic theory of the light, another study given origin to the . His work with his personal life and history made him a prominent figure in the XX century.

Timeline (1879-1955)

1878
Albert Einstein Born,infancy and study

Albert Einstein was born in Ulma, Germany to a working-class family on March 14, 1878.

Albert Einstein was from a jewish family. The father was Hermann Einstein, owner of a little farm producing electrical machinery and his mother was Pauline Koch. He spend his first years of life in Munich where received a rigid education form the Bavarian School System. Einstein never approved the traditional authoritarian scholastic system.

The Einstein moved freqeuntly (after Munich in Italy and Switzerland in Bern). He completed High School in Aarau (Switzerland), in the 1896 and after begun his study on physics and Mathematics at Zurich Politechnic which ended in July 1900.

1898
The Albert Einstein marriage

In the 1898, he meet Mileva Marić and they get marriage in the 1903. The couple had three son: Lieserl, in the 1902 who die after one year; Hans Albert in the 1904 and Eduard nel 1910. The marriage ended in 1919 and in the same year Einstein get marriage again with his cousing Elsa Einstein, which remain his companion until her dead in the 1936.

1905 the incredile year


After his bachelor Albert Einstein get a job in Berna at the Patent Office but in the same time published in the Annalen der Physik three original and hystorical work who were the begin of his academic career. The first job was about the double nature of light and lead to creation of quantistic theory.
The second work was about the atomic nature of matter.
The third was the basis of the theory of relaitivity and the famous E = mc2 formula was given as conseguence of this last work.

1905 - 1932
Einstein's chair and the Nobel Prize

The notable scientific production allowed Einstein to a number of chair by different University from Zurich (1909) to Praga (1911). In 1914 he went back to Berlin where he became Director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Institute untile the 1932 .

In 1921 he received the Nobel Prize on Physics for the law of Photoelectric effect (actually he get the prize an year later). In the motivation there wasn't reference on relativity even if that was the theory that give him fame around the world and outside the academic world.

1933 - 1945
The move to the United States

In 1933 he was forced by Nazi anti-Semitic persecutions to leave Germany and moved to the United States. Naturalized as an American citizen, he settled in Princeton where he taught Theoretical Physics at the University - at the Institute for Advanced Studies - until 1945, when he retired from academic activity.

After war and last years

After the Second World War, Einstein sought to promote peace in the world by promoting a real popular campaign against war and racist persecution. A week before his death in Princeton on April 17, 1955, together with seven other Nobel laureates, he compiled a pacifist declaration against nuclear weapons addressed to President Roosevelt: "We appeal as human beings to human beings: remember the your humanity and forget the rest. If you are able to do this, the way to a new paradise is open, otherwise the risk of universal death is before you” .

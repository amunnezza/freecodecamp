Albert Einstein was born in Ulma, Germany to a working-class family on March 14, 1878.

Albert Einstein was from a jewish family. The father was Hermann Einstein, owner of a litthe farm producing electrical machinery and his mother was Pauline Koch. He spend his first years of life in Munich where received a rigid education form the Bavarian School System. Einstein never approved the traditional authoritarian scholastic system.
